Formatting rules:

  • Comments are preserved
  • Only one blank line between two rulesets
  • Open curly brace is at the end of line, preceded by a space
  • Closed curly brace is at its own line
  • Each declaration inside the ruleset is indented with 4 spaces
  • There is no empty line between declarations inside a ruleset
  • Between property name and value, no space before colon and only one space after colon
  • Your indentations may be tabs or spaces
